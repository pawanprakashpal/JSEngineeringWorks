  2. Shaft Locking Housing

JS Engineering Works offers a comprehensive line of Keyless Locking Devices. Our Locking housing, commonly known as Shaft Locking Housing, connect hubs solidly to shafts, using a keyless mechanical interference fit, to transmit torque or to withstand axial thrust. Our Shaft Locking Housing offers great benefits over keyways locking devices including less machine time, easy installation and easy removal from shaft or hub offering easier maintenance.

Our Self-centering external Shaft Locking Housings are most recommended for hollow shafts. Mostly used in blowers, conveyors, cement mixers etc.
