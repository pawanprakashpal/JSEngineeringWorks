About

We "JS Engineering Works" are actively committed towards manufacturing and exporting a remarkable industrial solution. Founded in the year 1995 in Delhi, we are providing a remarkable and wide range of spare parts as per the global set standards. We are a proprietorship Company which is established with a motto of providing premium quality solutions in diverse specifications.

Our clients rely on us for technical know-how, superior quality products and complete peace of mind that we offer. Our state-of-the-art machinery and domain expertise help us to maintain a competitive edge in this exclusive domain. Our workforce is highly trained and experienced; thus, enabling them to adopt a professional approach very easily.

Under the direction of our mentor "Mr. Jai Prakash Pal", we have reached at the pinnacle of success. We are offering our products under the brand name "JSE". We are backed by state-of-the-art infrastructure and technically advanced tools and machinery that help us to offer quality products at competitive prices to the customers. Moreover, our robust infrastructure has helped us to garner reputation of being a reliable brand. We are committed to provide flawless quality products to our clients. All our products undergo stringent quality checks, under the supervision of quality controllers.
